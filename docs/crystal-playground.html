Action guide

Replace elementChanges one chemistry channel to the selected element, like SVGym element replacement.
Perturb latticeAdjusts the lattice constant and updates pseudo energy and reward.
Displace atomMoves atoms away from ideal fractional sites to visualize structural exploration.
Vacancy / insertionRemoves or inserts atoms to mimic defect and composition search.
RelaxMoves atoms back toward ideal sites and lowers the toy energy score.
